This platform aims to develop a platform dedicated to promoting direct sales of organic farm produce, facilitating a seamless connection between farmers and consumers. Traditionally, farmers sell their harvest to supermarkets or middlemen, often resulting in reduced profits and potential compromises in produce quality due to extended supply chains. This website will empower farmers by allowing them to directly upload their harvest details, set their own prices, and manage sales without intermediaries. Consumers will benefit from access to fresh, uncontaminated vegetables straight from the farm, ensuring higher quality and potentially lower costs.
